Casserole Recipe

Prep Time:

20 mins

Cook Time:

45 mins

Total Time:

1 hrs 5 mins

Servings:

12

Yield:

1 (9x13-inch) casserole

Ingredients

  • 4 cups peeled, cubed sweet potatoes
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Egg
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons butter, softened
  • ½ cup chopped pecans

Steps

  1. Cook and mash the sweet potatoes.
  2. Mix the casserole ingredients and transfer to a baking dish.
  3. Make the topping and sprinkle it over the sweet potato mixture.
  4. Bake until the topping is lightly browned.
